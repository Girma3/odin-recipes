Seven Layer Salad

Description

There's a reason this vintage seven layer salad recipes stood the test of time. it is easy to make, gorgeous and it will please everyone at your party.

Ingredients

    For the salad:
  • 1 pound of bacon
  • 1 large head iceberg lettuce-rinse, dried and chopped
  • 1 red onion,chopped
  • 1 (10 ounce) package green frozen peas thawed
  • 10 ounce shreddded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of chopped culiflowwer
    • Dressing:
  • 1 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2/3 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 tabelspoons white suger

Steps

  1. For the salad: palce the bacon in large skillet and cook in medium-high heat,turn occasionally untill it gets evenly browned for about 10 minutes.Drain bacon slice on paper towels; crumble and set aside.
  2. place chopped lattuce in large glass dish or bowl;top the layer with red onion,peas, shredded cheese, culiflowwer and bacon.
  3. For the dressing:whisk mayonnaise, parmesan cheese and sugar together in bowl untill smooth, Drizzle over salad and refrigerate untill chilled.
