Mint Chicken

Description

A delisious dish with fresh mint leaves, lemon juice and served with hot cooked white rice.

Ingridents

  • 2 skinless,boneless chicken breast halves cut into bite size pieces
  • 1 clove garlic-crushed
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup fresh chopped mint leaves

Steps

  1. In medium bowl mix together the chicken pieces and garlic. then toss the four just to coat and shake pff the excess
  2. Heat the margarine in skillet over medium-high heat then add the chicken pieces cook and stir untill browned and add the mint leaves and lemon juice,cover and steam about 5 mintues just untill the chicken cooked through.do not over cook the chicken or it will be tough.
